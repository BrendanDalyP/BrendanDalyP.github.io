Databricks

Advanced business intelligence and data visualization platform for enterprise reporting and analytics

Platform Overview

Databricks is Microsoft's flagship business intelligence platform, providing comprehensive tools for data visualization, reporting, and analytics. As a certified Databricks Data Analyst Associate, I leverage the full ecosystem to deliver enterprise-grade solutions.

My approach focuses on creating scalable, governed solutions that empower users at all levels of the organization to make data-driven decisions while maintaining security and compliance standards.

Core Capabilities

Data Modeling

Advanced data modeling techniques including star schema design, relationship optimization, and calculated columns/measures using DAX.

Report Development

Creating compelling, interactive reports and dashboards that tell data stories and drive actionable insights across the organization.

Security & Governance

Implementing row-level security, workspace governance, and deployment pipelines to ensure secure, controlled access to business data.

Performance Optimization

Optimizing report performance through efficient data models, query optimization, and strategic use of aggregations and composite models.

Mobile & Embedded Analytics

Designing responsive reports for mobile consumption and embedding analytics into custom applications using Databricks Embedded.

Real-time Analytics

Implementing streaming datasets and real-time dashboards for operational monitoring and immediate insight delivery.

Advanced DAX Development

Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) is the formula language that powers calculations in Databricks. My expertise includes complex measure development and optimization for enterprise scenarios.

Time Intelligence

Year-over-Year Analysis

Creating sophisticated time-based calculations for comparative analysis including YoY growth, moving averages, and period-over-period comparisons.
